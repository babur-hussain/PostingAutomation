Security & Trust

Safety Standards

Last updated: April 18, 2026

At Post Once, the safety and security of our users and their data are our highest priorities. We maintain rigorous standards to ensure a secure environment for social media automation.

1. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard encryption for all data at rest and in transit. Your social media access tokens are encrypted using AES-256 before being stored in our secure database.

2. Official API Usage

We only use official, verified APIs from platforms like Meta (Facebook, Instagram), Google (YouTube), and X (Twitter). We never ask for your passwords or bypass platform security measures.

3. Content Safety

Post Once is committed to preventing the spread of harmful content. We adhere strictly to the community guidelines of all connected platforms. Users are responsible for ensuring their content complies with:

  • Meta Community Standards
  • YouTube Community Guidelines
  • X Rules and Policies

4. Account Integrity

We monitor for unusual activity and employ rate-limiting to protect your social accounts from being flagged for spam. Our automation tools are designed to mimic natural posting patterns to maintain account health.

5. Vulnerability Management

Our systems undergo regular security audits and dependency updates to mitigate potential vulnerabilities. We follow the principle of least privilege, ensuring our application only requests the minimum necessary permissions to function.

6. Reporting Concerns

If you discover a security vulnerability or have concerns about safety, please contact our security team immediately at support@postingautomation.lfvs.in.
