Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to PostingAutomation. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our application and use our services.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you, including:

  • Identity Data: First name, last name, username or similar identifier.
  • Contact Data: Email address.
  • Social Media Data: Access tokens and profile information provided by Meta (Facebook/Instagram) when you connect your accounts to use our auto-posting features. We only use this data to perform explicitly requested actions, such as publishing posts on your behalf.

3. How We Use Your Data

We will only use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To register you as a new user.
  • To manage our relationship with you.
  • To provide our core service: scheduling and publishing content to your connected social media accounts using the official Meta APIs.

4. Data Security

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way, altered, or disclosed. Access tokens for social media platforms are encrypted at rest.

5. Data Deletion Request (User Rights)

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data and the revocation of any social media access tokens. You can disconnect your social media accounts directly within the app, which immediately deletes the stored access tokens. To request complete account deletion, please contact us.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact our support team.
