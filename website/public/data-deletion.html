Data Deletion Instructions

PostOnce is a social media management application that accesses your Facebook and Instagram accounts to help you publish content. We respect your privacy and provide a straightforward way to remove our application's access to your data and delete your information from our systems.

If you want to delete your data or remove our app's access to your Facebook/Instagram account, follow the instructions below.

Method 1: Removing Access via Facebook (Recommended)

You can completely remove our app's access directly from your Facebook settings. This will automatically revoke all permissions.

  1. Go to your Facebook account's Settings & Privacy.
  2. Click on Settings.
  3. In the left menu, scroll down and click on Apps and Websites.
  4. Find PostOnce in the list of active apps.
  5. Click the Remove button next to PostOnce.
  6. Confirm your choice by clicking Remove again in the prompt.

Once removed, we will no longer have access to your Facebook or Instagram data.

Method 2: Deleting Data via the PostOnce App

You can also disconnect your accounts directly from our mobile application, which will delete the stored access tokens from our database.

  1. Open the PostOnce mobile app.
  2. Navigate to the Accounts screen.
  3. Locate your connected Facebook or Instagram account.
  4. Tap the Disconnect button.
  5. Confirm the disconnection.

This action securely deletes your access tokens and account references from our servers.

Method 3: Requesting Complete Account Deletion

If you wish to have your entire PostOnce account and all associated data permanently deleted from our servers, you can contact our support team.

  1. Send an email to support@lfvs.in (or the email associated with your organization).
  2. Use the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
  3. Include the email address associated with your PostOnce account.
  4. We will process your request and permanently delete all your data within 7 business days.
